EIC Readiness Studio — Full Scope & Deliverables

EIC Readiness Studio is a tech proposal studio built for speed, evidence, and reviewer logic. We don’t “fill forms”. We help teams build an EIC-ready case (story + proof + execution) with clear scope, fast cycles, and high-quality QA.

Who this is for

  • Teams building a genuine innovation with a credible path to European/global scaling
  • Companies that can provide technical and business inputs quickly
  • Founders who want a structured, reviewer-aligned process (not generic grant writing)

1) EIC READY (7 days) — €1,200

Outcome A clear decision and plan before committing to a submission cycle.
  • GO/NO-GO verdict + EIC Fit Score
  • Evidence gap list (what is missing and why it matters)
  • Reviewer challenge list: the top objections reviewers will raise

Inputs required from you: existing deck/website (if any), short product description, team overview, and any validation/proof you have.

2) EIC SUBMIT-READY (Stage 1) — €4,900

Outcome Stage 1 submission assets that are reviewer-ready and easy to understand.
  • Stage 1 narrative: breakthrough + validation + impact
  • Pitch deck polish aligned with evaluator logic
  • Video pack:
    • script
    • Video Clarity Lab post-production (noise reduction, leveling, clarity enhancement, basic edit)
    • no synthetic voice, no avatars
  • Final QA pass + consistency check
  • Up to 2 revision rounds

Inputs required from you: technical description, validation proof (tests, pilots, users), market context, competitors, team bios.

3) EIC INVESTMENT-READY (Stage 2 — by invitation only) — €6,900 + success fee (agreed upfront)

Outcome A full proposal package built to investor-grade clarity and reviewer confidence.
  • Full proposal drafting across: Excellence / Impact / Implementation
  • Coherence Check: alignment of numbers, risks, work plan, and narrative across the entire package
  • Red-Team Review: evaluator-style scoring + “fatal flaws” list to close before final submission
  • Final QA pass + up to 2 revision rounds

Activation rule: Stage 2 work starts only if you are invited to submit Stage 2 after passing Stage 1.
