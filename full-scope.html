Full Scope & Deliverables

EIC Readiness Studio is a tech proposal studio built for speed, evidence, and reviewer logic. We don’t “fill forms”. We help teams build an EIC-ready case (story + proof + execution) with clear scope, fast cycles, and high-quality QA.

Who this is for

  • Teams building a genuine innovation with a credible path to European/global scaling
  • Companies that can provide technical and business inputs quickly
  • Founders who want a structured, reviewer-aligned process (not generic grant writing)

1) Diagnostic Sprint

€1,200

Credit policy

If we issue a GO and you proceed to EIC Submit-Ready within 30 days, the €1,200 Diagnostic Sprint fee is credited toward the Stage 1 package.

Outcome

A clear decision and plan before committing to a submission cycle.

Deliverables

  • GO / NO-GO decision + EIC Fit Score
  • Evidence gap analysis (what is missing and why it matters)
  • Action plan with next steps

Inputs required from you

Existing deck/website (if any), short product description, team overview, and any validation/proof you have.

2) EIC SUBMIT-READY (Stage 1)

€4,900

Outcome

Stage 1 submission assets that are reviewer-ready and easy to understand.

Deliverables

  • Stage 1 narrative for the short application form
  • Pitch deck content aligned with evaluator logic (storyline/structure)
  • Video pack: script + Video Clarity Lab post-production (noise reduction, levelling, clarity enhancement, basic edit)
  • Coherence Check (Stage 1): alignment across form, deck and video (claims, evidence, numbers, messaging)
  • Final QA pass + up to 2 revision rounds

Inputs required from you

Technical description, validation proof (tests, pilots, users), market context, competitors, team bios.

3) EIC INVESTMENT-READY (Stage 2 — by invitation only)

€6,900+ success fee (agreed upfront)

Outcome

A full proposal package built to investor-grade clarity and reviewer confidence.

Deliverables

  • Stage 2 full proposal, including the required financial information/plan
  • Coherence Check: alignment of numbers, risks, work plan, and narrative across the entire package
  • Final QA pass + up to 2 revision rounds

Activation rule

Stage 2 work starts only if you are invited to submit Stage 2 after passing Stage 1. Success fee is agreed upfront, case-by-case.
